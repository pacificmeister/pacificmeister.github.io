## 🛠️ Projects

### [⚡ Tow Boogie Builder](https://pacificmeister.github.io/towboogie-build/)
Complete DIY toolkit for building electric tow boggies. Four tools in one:
- **Build Configurator** — Pick parts, get a shopping list
- **Build Guide** — Step-by-step 4-day build sequence
- **Performance Calculator** — Physics-based speed, range, runtime estimates
- **Buyer's Guide** — Every tow boogie on the market, compared

Build for $750–$1,200 vs $7,750 commercial. [→ Launch](https://pacificmeister.github.io/towboogie-build/)

### [🏄 AXIS Foil Advisor](https://axis-advisor.vercel.app)
Foil selection wizard, product comparison, and anti-counterfeit center for AXIS foils.
- **Look-Up** — Instant specs for any AXIS product
- **Compare** — Side-by-side foil comparison
- **Wizard** — Personalized foil recommendations
- **Verify** — Anti-counterfeit authenticity checker

530+ rider feedback data points. [→ Launch](https://axis-advisor.vercel.app)

### [💬 FOIL.zone](https://foil.zone)
The world's largest DIY efoil community. 5,300+ members, 135,000+ posts, 9 years of open-source knowledge.

## 📖 Origin Story

**2008** — Jakob Kuttenkeuler invents the eFoil at KTH Stockholm (Evolo project)
**2016** — PacificMeister open-sources DIY electric hydrofoil designs on YouTube
**2017** — FOIL.zone founded — DIY community explodes worldwide
**2025** — AXIS Foil Advisor launches — community-powered foil intelligence
**2026** — Tow Boogie Builder launches — the next wave of open-source foiling tools
